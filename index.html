Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move my character?

Use the joystick in the bottom-left corner to move around the world.

How do I interact with creatures?

Press 'E' when near a creature to interact with them.

How do I use portals?

Approach a portal and press 'E' to teleport to different areas.

How do I play music?

Find the radio and press 'R' to open the music interface.

Who is Doofy?

Doofy is your friendly ghost puppy companion! Press 'W' to chat with him.

What is a Twisted?

In Dandy's World, a Twisted is a mysterious and potentially dangerous entity that randomly appears to challenge or threaten characters like Pebble. These dark, menacing creatures emerge unexpectedly, creating tension and excitement in the game.

  • Appearance: Dark, with glowing red eyes and a threatening demeanor
  • Behavior: Suddenly appears to create chaos or test the player's reflexes
  • Challenge: Players must quickly react to dismiss or avoid the Twisted
  • Game Mechanic: Adds an element of surprise and quick-time interaction
